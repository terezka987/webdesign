History and Benefits of Matcha

Matcha is a type of powdered green tea that originated in Japan. It has been consumed for centuries in traditional tea ceremonies. Today, it is popular worldwide for its unique flavor and numerous health benefits, including high antioxidant content and potential to boost metabolism and calm the mind.
